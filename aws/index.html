Amazon Web Services (AWS)

Amazon Web Services recently launched the AWS Academy, an integrated platform for hands-on labs, projects, and cloud-based practice. You will receive an invitation to AWS Academy directly from Instructure, the provider of the Canvas platform for the Academy. From there you will see our course and any available modules. Most importantly, the class I set up will credit your student account with $100, so I anticipate no student cost for this part of the class.

If you have an existing AWS account you are welcome to use it. We will discuss AWS account options the first week of class.

Caution about AWS billing!

YOU, and only you, are responsible for charges to your AWS account(s). The instructor will not be held responsible for resources left running, or for large "surprise" charges to your account. You will be instructed in how to set a monthly budget for your account and receive notifications when your expenses cross thresholds nearing that budget.
