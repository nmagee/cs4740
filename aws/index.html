Amazon Web Services (AWS)

Given that AWS is currently shifting how it supports "classrooms" and free student accounts, this course requires that you create your own AWS account. This requires a credit card, but all new accounts are placed in the "free tier" which means that most all operations this semester you in your AWS account will incur little to no charges.

If you have an existing AWS account you are welcome to use it, however it may no longer eligible for the free tier. At the end of the semester it is your choice whether you would like to keep your account or delete it.

Caution about AWS billing!

YOU, and only you, are responsible for charges incurred in this account. The instructor will not be held responsible for resources left running, or for large "surprise" charges to your account. You will be trained in how to set a monthly budget for your account and receive notifications when your actual expenses cross thresholds nearing your monthly budget.

Students should complete this setup and be able to access the AWS Console by the end of the first week of class.
